Organization Overview

TESRECO is dedicated to nurturing young talent by providing practical learning experiences, industry exposure, and mentorship opportunities. Through its internship program, students gain hands-on experience and develop skills that prepare them for successful professional careers.

Internship Domains

Web Development
Python Development
Data Science
Artificial Intelligence

Mission

To empower students and aspiring professionals with practical knowledge, technical expertise, and real-world experience that bridges the gap between academics and industry.

Vision

To create a future-ready workforce by fostering innovation, continuous learning, and professional excellence through impactful internship opportunities.
